Discover our gnome origin...

On our travels across Europe, particularly through German Christmas Markets, we discovered the delightfullness of Christmas Gnome décor. It didn't take us long to realize these quaint, handcrafted gnomes would be a star in anyones home...for any holiday. After years of research, design work, and practice, Stuttgart Gnomes is ready to bring this European tradition to your home. We offer unique designs for all seasons and are constantly creating new designs for your home.
